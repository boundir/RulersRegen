[h1]Description[/h1]

Rulers will now regen Health and Armor when leaving the combat zone.

[h1]Changes[/h1]

Rulers will regen 50% of their health and 100% of their armor.
Amount of regen can be customized in [u]XComRulersRegen.ini[/u]
